About

mochiOS is a lightweight operating system project that focuses on stability, performance, and elegant design.

The project is primarily developed by middle-school students, who value learning, craftsmanship, and practical engineering.

Mission

Build a crash-resistant, high-performance, and elegant operating system that is easy to learn and use.

Vision

Empower the next generation of developers by providing a well-designed platform where learning, experimentation, and performance matter.

Values

Elegance & Integration

We pursue beauty in code and seamless integration—small, dependable primitives composed into a polished whole.

Open & Flat Community

We foster an environment where everyone's voice matters and learning is encouraged—even for our youngest contributors.

Autonomy & Responsibility

Members take ownership and ship reliable code—prioritizing stability and performance.
